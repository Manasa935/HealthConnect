Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I register?

To register, click on the "Register" link on the homepage and fill out the required information.

2. How do I book an appointment?

After logging in, navigate to the "Hospitals" section, select a hospital, and choose a doctor to book an appointment.

3. What should I do if I forget my password?

Click on the "Forgot Password?" link on the login page, and follow the instructions to reset your password.

4. How can I contact support?

You can reach out to our support team using the contact form below or email us at healthconnectedgmail.com.
